He rots down in dark prison cell 
Though visited by the Prince himself 
High majesty falls to his knees 
He fills night castle with sorrow and pleads 

 
He cries: 
"Forgive me brother, i've never wanted to be where I am now 
Stupid borders were built upon this cursed crown 
I have all I've desired
From the childhood you've inspired me to dream big, 
But I dream back
I regret every part of my plan
I feel an impossible pain 

 
I have fallen down so many layers 
I'm beat by the liars 
You the last one I can trust on 
Hearing my soul, looking into my turmoil" 

 
He rots down in dark prison cell 
Though visited by the Prince himself 
The silence of his answer 
It burns heart sharper than any razor.
